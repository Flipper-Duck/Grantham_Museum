Bring your class to Grantham Museum and discover the real thing. We are housed in a beautiful 1926 Carnegie Library building right in the heart of Grantham. Admission is free — the only cost to your school is transport.

What your class will experience

  • Discover Grantham's greats — Isaac Newton, Margaret Thatcher, and Edith Smith, Britain's first female police officer with powers of arrest
  • Explore original objects and artefacts from across history in a genuine museum setting
  • See the 1950s Grantham scale model and the historic apothecary shop
  • Step inside a genuine 1926 Carnegie Library building
  • Free admission for all — only transport to cover
  • Accessible building with step-free routes and facilities
